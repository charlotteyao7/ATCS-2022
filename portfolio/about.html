Hi! I'm Charlotte Yao.

I am a senior at Menlo School in Atherton, California, and I am self-taught artist. I've never taken an official art course so my experience is largely experimental. I enjoy watercolor, acrylics, and line art, but my all-time favorite medium is color pencil. I love the control I have when I hold the pencil, and I find the process of layering and burnishing very theraputic. Whenever I am overwhelmed with school work, I sit down at my desk with my color pencils and draw something that inspires me. My subjects mostly include nature and food, and I try to capture their detailed textures.
